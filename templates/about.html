About QUENCH

What is QUENCH?

QUENCH is a smart IoT-based water dispensing system that uses real-time UPI payment verification, sensor integration, and dashboard analytics to deliver water efficiently. Built by engineers, for the future 💧

It stood for Quality based Unique Electronic Networked Control for Hydration.

Built by engineers to tackle the problems related to water wastage and contactless dispensing payment solutions.
